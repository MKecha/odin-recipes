Omelet

Description

This is an omelet. I don't know how to cook and, honestly, I've never made one that looked even remotely as edible as the one in the picture.

You usually eat it in the morning. You know one of these mornings where, for some reason, you wake and feel like your life isn't a fuck up? Where you think to yourself: "today is the day my life gets better"? This omelet is the perfect way of reminding yourself that you are worthless. If you can't make an omelet successfully, why would you even think that you can fix your life? Anyway, here's what's you'll need.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs;
  • 1/2 tbsp. of butter;
  • 1/3 cup of diced onions;
  • 1 pinch of salt;
  • 1 pinch of black pepper;
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon of fresh coriander.

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs in a seperate bowl and mix throughly until contents become a uniform mixture;
  2. Cut the coriander finely (Is that a word? I don't know) and add them to the bowl;
  3. Bring the pan to medium temperature;
  4. Insert the butter and let it melt. Swirl the pan around to make sure the butter covers the entirety of the pan;
  5. Add the mixture;
  6. Let it cook for 5 minutes, then add salt and pepper and fold in half;
  7. Your failed omelet is ready, time to give up and go to bed after having taken a single bite. You weren't hungry anyways.
