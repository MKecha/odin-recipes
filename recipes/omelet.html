Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 tbsp. of butter
  • 1/3 cup of diced onions
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of black pepper
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon of fresh coriander

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs in a seperate bowl and mix throughly until contents become uniform
  2. Cut the coriander finely (Is that a word? I don't fucking know, man) and add them to the bowl
  3. Bring pan to medium temperature
  4. Insert the butter and let it melt. Swirl the pan around to make sure the butter covers the entirety of the pan
  5. Add the mixture
  6. Let it cook for 5 minutes, then add salt and pepper and fold in half
  7. Your failed omelet is ready, time to given up and go to bed after having taken a single bite. You weren't hungry anyways.
